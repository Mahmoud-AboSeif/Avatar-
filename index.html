avatar

Avatar(marketed as James Cameron's Avatar) is a 2009 epic fantasy science fiction film directed, written, co-produced, and co-edited by James Cameron and starring Sam Worthington and Zoe Saldana as the title characters, with Stephen Lang, Michelle Rodriguez,[6] and Sigourney Weaver in supporting roles. It is the first installment in the Avatar film series. It is set in the mid-22nd century.
